Looking for ways to earn money?

AC Jobs is the place to find jobs and offer home services in the Alameda County. This app was the Innovator prize winner at the Alameda County App Challenge Hackathon.


Multi Platform

Downloadable on the App Store (iOS), Google Play (Android) and it has its Web version.

Community Friendly

AC Jobs services creates an interaction between the residents of a certain neighborhood, and help the community grow.

Intuitive Interface

Easy access sections, clean design and easy to use.

Application Features

  • Find Jobs

    ACJobs pulls jobs listings from the internet and relate them to the Alameda County’s “Certified Green Business” dataset. Afterwards, the information is made available via an intuitive interface in a column view.

  • Spot Companies

    Using ESRI maps services we are able to plot a map with all the companies in the Alameda County area and check their respective job oportunities.

  • Post Services

    The application even allows people to post opportunities to earn money locally via more informal services like walking dogs and doing yard work.
